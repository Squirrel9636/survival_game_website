SURVIVAL GAME

Summary

This a new take on the survival game genre.The players goal is to conquer the island they are in.The island is dominated by prehistoric dinosaurs.The player must defeat the dinos to survive or possibly befriend them. If you manage to befriend the dinosaurs they are pretty much half way there.Befriending or taming dinos is really helpful to the survivor.They provide tons of utility.

Gameplay

The game starts of with you stranded on an island. Moments later you realize that the island contains prehistoric animals.Some of which are friendly while others,not so much.So you start getting new resources maybe by hitting rocks or cutting trees. You start to make new equipments and buildings.After making a good weapon you try to fight one of the dinos but you may die and lose everything.But that should not stop you from striving for survival.Another element of the game is taming which is explained briefly in section below.Its a mechanism to make friends with the creatures.

The idea of taming creatures

To survive the elements the players must defeat other creatures or take another route and actually turn the elements into their friends.This is where taming comes in.If the player just like in modern life gives food
